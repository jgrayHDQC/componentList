How to Prepare For Your Installation  
 
  • Disassemble beds, remove bed coverings and any items underneath.
  • Clear all surfaces and floor spaces, remove pictures and mirrors from walls, and put away small items such as china, knick-knacks and books.
  • Clear closets of low hanging clothes - clothes must be at least 4“ above the floor.
  • Disconnect and move all electronics, such as TVs and computers.
 
For more detailed information on preparing your home for installation see our Flooring Installation Preparation service page.
